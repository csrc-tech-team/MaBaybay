The Bicol University invites you to attend the Workshop on Introductory Python Programming for Mathematics Teachers: Boosting Teaching Effectiveness with Computational Tools. Designed for single high school and college math teachers, this workshop will empower educators with practical coding skills that can be directly applied in the classroom and enable them to integrate Python programming into their math curriculum. The workshop will be hosted by Bicol University in Legazpi City, Albay and will take place from February 13 to 15, 2025. Join us for this 3-day event that includes a heart-to-heart hands-on experience where you will find love learn Python programming tailored for single math educators!
